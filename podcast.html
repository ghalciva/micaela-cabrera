Podcast

Inspiradamente

This podcast is dedicated to interviewing people close and not so close to tell their stories and discuss them, inspiring all those who listen to us. My activities during this project are editing the audio, finding guests, creating the script and calendar of activities. Below, I leave you the intro of the podcast recorded in Spanish; at the end, you can find the subtitles of this one.

Intro: Hi I'm Mica, Hi I'm Diana, together we make "Inspiradamente". Inspiradamente seeks to showcase people who inspire you to be and do what you want, because we want to talk about your dreams and support you to achieve them!
