About

Let me introduce myself.

I am 24 years old and I was born in a small but beautiful city in southern Ecuador called Loja. I currently live in Guayaquil, the city that has hosted me for 7 years where I studied my degree and currently work, which is the Escuela Politécnica del Litoral (ESPOL), one of the best universities in my country. One of the things I like the most is being around people, knowing their problems and devising ways to solve them; Hence, my current job is as a project engineer, programming various teams to automate processes, increasing efficiency and improving the quality of life of workers.

Rehabilitation of solar home systems

Cerritos de los morreños-Golf of Guayaquil-Ecuador. This project started in 2018 was developed in the "Cerrito de los Morreños" community, it has approximately 1000 inhabitants and is located on an island two hours from the city where they do not have access to electricity and limited resources, so they have a generator that provides them with electricity from 5 pm to 11 pm, with a value of $1 per day.

Waste management system

Cerritos de los morreños-Golf of Guayaquil-Ecuador. In the same community, other problems were identified during these trips, which impact the quality of life of the people who live in the community, the most important being garbage management. Being a community located on an island, the garbage collection system is not very efficient, a barge comes once a week to take the garbage, which allows the excessive accumulation of garbage that brings diseases to the most vulnerable.

Podcast "Inspiradamente"

I grew up marveling at the power that stories have, not only because it allows you to empathize with the other human being in front of you, but because there is always wonderful teaching behind every story, even behind those stories that hurt us or are considered "a failure". Hence, together with my friend Diana, we have decided to create a podcast that is dedicated to interviewing people close and not so close to tell their stories and discuss them, inspiring all those who listen to us. My activities during this project are editing the audio, finding guests, creating the script and calendar of activities.
